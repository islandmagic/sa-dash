BREAKING / KĪLAUEA COMMUNITY SAFETY UPDATE

Kauaʻi police have closed Kīlauea Road between Kolo Road and Kaikala Street due to an active police investigation. Hawaii News Now is reporting this as an ongoing homicide investigation.

Neighbors in the area report a heavy police presence, evacuations of nearby homes, and continued road closures. Several claims are circulating online about shots fired, a barricaded person, hostages, and a possible connection to a Hanalei incident, but those details have not been confirmed by KPD in the public reporting available at this time.

Please avoid the area, do not drive by to observe, and use alternate routes. Do not post names, exact house locations, photos of police positions, or unverified suspect details, as that could endanger residents, officers, or anyone involved.

Confirmed as of now:

1. Kīlauea Road is closed between Kolo Road and Kaikala Street.
2. Police are asking the public to avoid the area.
3. The investigation is active and ongoing.
4. HNN reports it is a homicide investigation.
5. Many neighborhood details remain unverified.

Monitor KPD, County of Kauaʻi, Kauai Now, and Hawaii News Now for official updates.
